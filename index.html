Yes. R150/month on the Starter plan covers your hosting, your site, and basic support. No setup fee. No surprise charges. The price you see is what you pay.
What if I'm not tech-savvy at all? +
Perfect — you don't need to be. Everything happens over WhatsApp. You send us your details, we build the site. You won't need to touch any code, ever.
What if I want to make changes later? +
WhatsApp us. Business plan includes 1 free update per month. Pro plan includes unlimited small updates. Bigger changes (new pages, new sections) are quoted separately but always fairly.
Will my site actually rank on Google? +
We set up all your on-page SEO basics from day one — proper titles, descriptions, Google Business Profile, and fast loading speeds. You won't be invisible. Local SEO results typically show within 4–8 weeks.
Can I cancel if I'm not happy? +
Absolutely. No contracts, no lock-in. Cancel any month with a WhatsApp message. We'd rather earn your loyalty than trap you into it.
What kinds of businesses do you work with? +
Barbers, salons, restaurants, mechanics, electricians, tutors, boutiques, fitness trainers, photographers — any local SA business that wants to be found online. If you have customers, we can help.
